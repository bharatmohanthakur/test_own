## Top Public Notebook Analysis: konbu17 (0.72 score)
**"Nemotron SFT LoRA with CoT-Selected Data"** — updated 1 day ago

### Config:
- LoRA: rank=32, alpha=32 (NOT 64), 4 targets (in_proj|out_proj|up_proj|down_proj)
- Training: 2 epochs, LR=1e-4, batch=1, grad_accum=8, max_seq_len=4096
- Uses SFTTrainer from trl (not custom loop)
- gradient_checkpointing=True, use_reentrant=False
- bf16=True

### Data Strategy (KEY INSIGHT):
- Generated CoT via LLM inference on all 9500 problems
- **Rule-based correctness filtering**: only train on examples where model gets right answer
- **Type-based sampling** (not equal per type):
  - Bit Manipulation: 607 (all available)
  - Unit Conversion: 700
  - Text Encryption: 700
  - Gravitational Constant: 400
  - Numeral Conversion: 300
  - Equation Transformation: 200 (all available)
  - Total: ~2907 training examples

### Cipher Trick:
- Embedded 77-word Alice's Wonderland dictionary into cipher prompts
- Since cipher puzzles use monoalphabetic substitution with fixed vocabulary,
  providing the encrypted→plain mapping helps enormously
